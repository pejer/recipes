# Pour over

A few hints & tricks.

## Washed coffee recipe

- 15g coffee
- grind: 32 clicks
- 250g water
- 93°C
- 1st pour 50g
- 5 pours of 40g each

## Natural funky coffee recipe

- 16g coffee
- grind: 28 clicks
- 250g water
- 90°C
- 1st pour 70g
- 2 pours of 90g each

Natural coffee uses reduced agitation technique to prevent over-extraction and astringenc
