# Pour over

A few hints & tricks.

## Washed coffee recipe

- 15g coffee
- grind: 32 clicks
- 250g water
- 93°C
- 6 pours of 40g each

## Natural funky coffee recipe

- 16g coffee
- grind: 28 clicks
- 250g water
- 90°C
- 3 pours of 70g
- 90g
- 90g

Natural coffee uses reduced agitation technique to prevent over-extraction and astringenc
